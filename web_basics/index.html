Caracals!

Caracals look sort of like housecats, except they have really cool, pointy ear fur.

They not only look like housecats, but they are closely related to them, too:

  1. Kingdom: Animalia
  2. Phylum: Chordata
  3. Class: Mammalia
  4. Order: Carnivora
  5. Family: Felidae
  6. Subfamily: Felinae
  7. Genus: Caracal
  8. Species: C. Caracal

There are seven subspecies of caracal (Thanks, Wikipedia!)

  • C. c. algira
  • C. c. caracal
  • C. c. damarensis
  • C. c. limpopoensis
  • C. c. lucani
  • C. c. nubica
  • C. c. poecilotis
  • C. c. schmitzi
